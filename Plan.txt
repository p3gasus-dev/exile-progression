EXILE PROGRESSION (fork of Exile Leveling)
Relies on heartofphos/exile-leveling for the core ACT1-10 ROUTE

LAYOUT:
	NAVBAR: [ DASHBOARD | ROUTE | CAMPAIGN | ATLAS | BUILD | SETTINGS ]
	DASHBOARD: ROUTE PROGRESS, UNIQUE ITEMS, MISC.
	ROUTE: ACT1-10, VOIDSTONE1-4 , CHALLENGE1-40
	CAMPAIGN: ACT1-10 SETTINGS, CAMPAIGN SPECIFIC SETTINGS
	ATLAS: ATLAS SETTINGS
	BUILD: BUILD IMPORT, BUILD SETTINGS
	SETTINGS: EDIT ROUTE, 3RD-PARTY EXPORT, PROJECT ON GITHUB

DESC:
	3RD-PARTY EXPORT: Export route for use outside of Exile Progression.
	ACT1-10: Same as original repo. Now has our additions added.
	ATLAS: Options related to the ATLAS
	ATLAS SETTINGS: Settings related to ATLAS POINTS, VOIDSTONE1-4, ETC.
	BUILD: Settings related to UNIQUES, SPECIAL MODS, ANOINTS, PANTHEON, ETC.
	BUILD IMPORT: Imports build info.
	CAMPAIGN SETTINGS: Settings related to ACT1-10.
	CHALLENGE1-40: Fastest/easiest way to completing each challenge. Auto tracks challenges completed during ACT1-10 and VOIDSTONE1-4.
	DASHBOARD: Shows overall progression of BUILD.
	EDIT ROUTE: Option to Edit/Export route.
	MISC.: Shows specifcs such as ANOINTS, SPECIAL MODS, ETC.
	PROJECT ON GITHUB: Link to GITHUB Repo.
	ROUTE: Tracking progress of player from early game to endgame.
	ROUTE PROGRESS: Shows progress of current ACT, VOIDSTONE, or CHALLENGE.
	SETTINGS: Settings related to Exile Progression functions.
	UNIQUE ITEMS: Track IMPORT BUILD->UNIQUE ITEMS and where/how they can be acquired.
	VOIDSTONE1-4: Fastest/easiest route to completing voidstones 1, 2 ,3 ,4.
