#section Maven Witnessing
{generic|Activate Maven witnessing on your Map Device (press the Maven icon when opening a map)}
{generic|Defeat map bosses while Maven is witnessing — she collects each boss you kill}
{generic|After 3 witnessed bosses you receive Maven's Invitation: The Atlas}
{generic|Enter the Maven's Crucible via the invitation — defeat all bosses simultaneously for Crescent Splinters}
{generic|Repeat Maven invitations until you have 10 Crescent Splinters}
{generic|10 Crescent Splinters automatically combine into Maven's Writ}

#section The Maven
{generic|Use an Orb of Alchemy on Maven's Writ to make it Rare (improves loot)}
{generic|Place Maven's Writ in your Map Device and enter The Maven's Realm}
{kill|The Maven}
{generic|Take the Ceremonial Voidstone and socket it into your Atlas Map Device}
