#section Maven Witnessing
Activate {league|Maven} witnessing on your Map Device (press the Maven icon when opening a map)
Defeat map bosses while {league|Maven} is witnessing — she collects each boss you kill
After 3 witnessed bosses you receive {quest_text|Maven's Invitation: The Atlas}
Enter the {arena|Maven's Crucible} via the invitation — defeat all bosses simultaneously for {quest_text|Crescent Splinters}
    #sub All bosses are added simultaneously — kill them quickly before Maven's memory fails
Repeat until you have 10 {quest_text|Crescent Splinters}
    #sub 10 Crescent Splinters automatically combine into {quest_text|Maven's Writ}

#section The Maven
Alch the {quest_text|Maven's Writ} to Rare (improves loot)
➞ {arena|The Maven's Realm}
{kill|The Maven}
Take the {quest_text|Ceremonial Voidstone} and socket it into your Atlas Map Device
