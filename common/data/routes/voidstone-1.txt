#section Early Atlas
Talk to {quest_text|Kirac} in Oriath to unlock the Atlas
    #sub Complete Acts 1-10 first if you haven't already
Run white maps (T1-T5) and complete Kirac's opening quest chain
{league|The Envoy} will appear in a map — speak with it to unlock Eldritch Influence
Talk to Kirac to receive the {league|Eater of Worlds} and {league|Searing Exarch} icons on your Map Device

#section Eater of Worlds Progression
Activate {league|Eater of Worlds} influence on your Map Device when opening maps
Run T1-T4 influenced maps — complete the biome objective in each
Run T5-T8 influenced maps — complete the biome objective in each
Run T9-T12 influenced maps — complete the biome objective in each
Run T13-T15 influenced maps until a {quest_text|Screaming Invitation} drops from a map boss

#section The Eater of Worlds
Alch the {quest_text|Screaming Invitation} to Rare (improves loot)
➞ {arena|The Absence of Symmetry and Harmony}
{kill|The Eater of Worlds}
Take the {quest_text|Grasping Voidstone} and socket it into your Atlas Map Device
